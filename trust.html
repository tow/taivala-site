Trust & Security

At Taivala, security isn’t an afterthought—it’s the foundation.

Our platform is designed for enterprise use from day one, with secure development practices, audited architecture, and built-in identity controls.

We combine deep cybersecurity experience with a modern engineering approach to deliver software you can trust in production environments.

Security Leadership

Our founding team has spent decades building and securing internet-scale platforms. Those lessons inform every product decision.

Founded by the former CTO of Finland’s leading cybersecurity company.

Supported by independent advisors across incident response and compliance.

Secure Architecture & Development

Security is built in from the first line of code.

All features go through a secure SDLC process: threat modeling, eview, dependency scanning, and environment isolation.

Continuous application security test coverage and supply-chain integrity checks are standard.

Enterprise Identity

Single Sign-On (SSO) and SAML are standard. Tenants integrate with your existing IdP (Microsoft Entra ID, Google Workspace, etc.) from day one—no extra contracts, no additional SKU.

Just-in-time provisioning with SCIM. Granular role mappings enforce least-privilege access.

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) policies are inherited directly from your IdP.

Compliance Roadmap

Taivala is committed to enterprise-grade assurance.

Our ISO 27001 and SOC 2 programs are being established in partnership with independent auditors and security consultants.

These frameworks guide our current controls and continuous improvement approach as we prepare formal certification.
